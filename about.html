ABOUT ME

Ever since I was young my father and I always spent our spare time drawing together, studying comics, and watched various animated shows//movies. We were both enfatuated with the art styles and visual interpretations of these stories. Ever since, I've been obsessed with being able replicate the same feeling I experienced. Now, as a graphic designer, I can interperet, break down, and simplify a variety of different ideas, and synthesize these concepts into a graphic that is more publically understandable.
